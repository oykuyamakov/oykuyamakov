    • >Algorave Istanbul 1.18.25

      An audio-visual performance by yuko, Uzak, Arian, and Baramuk, performed at nohextended.


      Audio-reactive point-cloud data, edited live, was transferred to Unity via Spout. Unity's powerful HDRP was utilized to craft VFX with texture 2D point-cloud data, enhanced by post-processing and lighting effects.

      A live-edited Stable Diffusion texture from TouchDesigner, transmitted over the local network, was integrated into the Unity 3D scene as a backdrop, seamlessly blending various media into a cohesive composition.

    • >Gurbet - Alter Egos: Audio Visual Showcase | NYU ITP

      Gurbet is an audio-visual performance by Yuko, presented at the "Alter Egos: Audio Visual Showcase" hosted by Ali Santana at NYU ITP.

      Developed in Unity, gurbet combines custom HLSL shaders, URP post-processing, and a unique data-moshing shader integrated through custom render passes.

      By utilizing camera motion vectors, the shaders seamlessly merge the 3D environment with a background video, creating a dynamic data moshing-effect. The video backdrop features a 140 Journos documentary.

      Music: Hey Douglas - Deterjan.

    • >The Cycle - Aalto University

      The Cycle is an immersive experience exploring the interplay between human nature and water cycles through film, installation, and performance.

      The installation, created by Yiko and Ziyu, uses TouchDesigner and Azure Kinect to visualize the water cycle's various phases. By manipulating noise patterns, participants interact with and experience different states of water. The accompanying film was shot by Saranya, Ziga, Oyku, and Ziyu in Helsinki.
