    • Merge Barista

      Merge Barista is a mobile merge game available on Android and iOS platforms. Merge games challenge players to combine matching items to create higher-tier objects, progress through levels, and unlock rewards in an engaging puzzle-based experience.

      I contributed to this project as a Game Programmer at Zerosum, working with an exceptional team over the course of a year. My key responsibilities included:

      • Addressables System Implementation: Implemented Unity's Addressables system to efficiently manage and deliver large-scale data updates and assets, optimizing performance and scalability.
      • Live Operations: Supported live game updates, ensuring smooth integration of new content, features, and bug fixes to enhance player satisfaction.
      • System Architecture: Designed and developed modular systems for managing game state, dynamic content, and progression logic.

      Achievements: The project was successfully acquired by Rollic, a subsidiary of Zynga, marking a significant milestone for the team.

      Check out Merge Barista on the App Store and Google Play.

    • Belgrad Park is a proof-of-concept project exploring a real-time-play horror game in a confined space. You are a true-crime podcaster investigating an abandoned Ranger Station in upstate New York where, twenty years ago, three park employees mysteriously vanished. In search of evidence that might bring this cold case back to life, you broke into the park for an overnight investigation and found yourself trapped in a never-ending time loop. Can you survive, escape the station, and uncover the terrible truth about what happened all those years ago?

      I worked on this project as a Game Designer, and Game Programmer collaborating with a team of 3. Belgrad Park was showcased at NYU Spring Showcase 2024
