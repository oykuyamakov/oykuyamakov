    • Merge Barista

      Merge Barista is a mobile merge game available on Android and iOS platforms. Merge games are puzzle-based experiences where players combine matching items to create higher-tier objects, progressing through levels and unlocking rewards.

      I worked on this project as a Game Programmer at Zerosum, collaborating with a talented team over the course of a year. My responsibilities included:

      • Addressables System Implementation: Utilized Unity's Addressables system to manage large-scale data updates and optimize asset delivery efficiently.
      • Live Operations: Supported live game updates, ensuring seamless integration of new content and fixes to enhance the player experience.
      • System Architecture: Designed and implemented robust systems for managing game state and content dynamically.

      The project was a significant success and was eventually acquired by Rollic, a subsidiary of Zynga. You can find Merge Barista on the App Store and Google Play.

    • Belgrad Park

      Belgrad Park is a proof-of-concept project exploring a real-time-play horror game in a confined space. You are a true-crime podcaster investigating an abandoned Ranger Station in upstate New York where, twenty years ago, three park employees mysteriously vanished. In search of evidence that might bring this cold case back to life, you broke into the park for an overnight investigation and found yourself trapped in a never-ending time loop. Can you survive, escape the station, and uncover the terrible truth about what happened all those years ago?

      I worked on this project as a Game Designer, and Game Programmer collaborating with a team of 3. Belgrad Park was showcased at NYU Spring Showcase 2024
